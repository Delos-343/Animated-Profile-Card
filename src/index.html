FIRST ORDER


T-365 x 2 : The Day

An autocratic military junta that was formed from the remnants of the Galactic Empire during the height of the New Republic Era; the First Order, or simply the "Order", claimed legitimacy as a successor state to the predating Empire.

As a polity, the First Order was ruled by a Supreme Leader from a mobile headquarters instead of a planetary capital. It grew from a hermit state to a galactic power, having gradually colonized sectors of the Unknown Regions and boosted by secessionists from the New Republic, annexing territories that would serve a crucial role in the regime's grand plan to seize the galaxy at large.

Yet despite their unrelenting conquest of major star systems, the First Order and its rule of iron fist was met with fierce resistance and rebellion.
